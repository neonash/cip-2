How to use Result Clustering

  • This chart can be used to understand groups of related topics (or clusters) covered within the dataset
  • Depending on the quality of the input documents, some clusters may not make much sense
  • Each cluster is assigned a label for readability, that can be clicked to see reviews pertaining to that cluster
  • Some content may not be clustered at all; these will be assigned to the synthetic 'Other Topics' cluster
